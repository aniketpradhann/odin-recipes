Chole Bhature

Description

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature). It's known for its rich flavors and hearty texture.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green chilies
  • Spices:
    • Cumin powder
    • Coriander powder
    • Turmeric powder
    • Garam masala
    • Chilli powder
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Flour (for Bhature)
  • Yogurt (for Bhature)
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight.
  2. Boil the chickpeas until soft.
  3. Saut√© onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies in oil.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add spices and cooked chickpeas.
  6. Simmer until the curry thickens.
  7. Mix flour, yogurt, salt, and water to make a dough.
  8. Let the dough rest for a few hours.
  9. Roll the dough into small circles.
  10. Deep fry the circles until golden brown.
  11. Serve hot Bhature with Chole.
